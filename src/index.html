Trade Reporting

For the purposes of this POC, there is an expectation that some other party would bring the buyer and seller together and report the pre-matched trade to the DVP platform. Also, applicable for this test is determining if it is doable to create a simple dashboard for an administrator to track the progression of trades in the system in order to deal with the exceptions.

Trade Reporting

We try to prove that an autherised trade reporting party can be used to report trades, but the confirmations must still come from the buyer and seller before an atomic swap is done.

Trade Tracking

Confirmations And Mandates

The major proof point is to see if we can give ultimate control of who is allowed to confirm on a beneficial holder's accound back to the beneficial holder to assign, and revoke, mandates as they please.

Empower

Can we create a mechanism where the client has the power to confirm his/her own trades?

Entrust

End investors often do not want the burden of managing their own portfolio, so can we allow for the user to give the role of confirming his trade leg to his service provider of choice?

Revoke

Even though an investor has allowed his service provider to confirm trade legs on their behalf, can the investor still retain the ability to revoke the service provider's authority, thus retaining ultimate control?

Settlement

Have we achieved an "All or Nothing" mechanism ensuring the whole trade, or no part of the trade, settles to completion? Yes, with the bonus of a reduced need for intermediaries, less cost for the full trade lifecycle, increased speed, and empowering the investor to a greater extent than the current landscape allows for.
